About Us

Smile Charities is an NGO working since 1984 in the fields of Social, Rural, Health, Education, Environment, Agriculture, Women and Child Development. Our overall goal is the empowerment of people from poor and marginalized communities, thereby improving their lives and livelihoods. We are the architects of the government vision of sustainable development under this umbrella, we have organized various development projects, workshops, training camps and programs etc.

Our Mission

We want a country of hope, tolerance and social justice, where the problem of people is overcome and people live in dignity and security.

Our Vision

Smile Charities helps reduce social exclusion by accelerating education, health and empowerment for people in rural areas.

Our Objectives

Handicraft

To make disabled, widow and old women self-sufficient by arranging chilis and other industries.

Working Women

Crash planning for raising children of working women.

Education

To make educated unemployed men and women self-sufficient by arranging small scale industries according to their interest.

Family Welfare

Adopt family welfare programs and establish and operate Datavam Dispensary Maternity Home, Choka Center, etc.
